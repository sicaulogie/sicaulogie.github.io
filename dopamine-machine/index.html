Dopamine Timer

The idea of the Dopamine Machine is that your dopamine is released by arbituary use of things that satisfy you easily such as mini games, social media apps, etc.

In this program, the time you spend to set your dopamine level back to normal is equivalent to the time you spent on those platforms. Click "charge" to count the time you have wasted, and "discarge" to put down your device and wait for it to finish.

More features may be added in the future.
